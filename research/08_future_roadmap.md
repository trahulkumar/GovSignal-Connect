# Future Research Roadmap

## Phase 2: Semantic Intelligence (Q3 2026)
- **Vector Database:** Implement Pinecone/Milvus to store embeddings of Federal Register documents.
- **RAG Architecture:** Replace KDA with Retrieval-Augmented Generation for semantic matching.

## Phase 3: Autonomous Negotiation (Q1 2027)
- **Bid-Bot:** An agent that not only identifies opportunities but drafts responses to RFPs.
- **Blockchain Escrow:** Smart contracts for automated fund release upon milestone verification.

## Phase 4: National Deployment
- **FedRAMP Certification:** Hardening the platform for storage of CUI (Controlled Unclassified Information).
- **Inter-Agency Data Sharing:** Cross-correlating DoD and DoE demand signals.
